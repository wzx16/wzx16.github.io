About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the department of computer science at Purdue University advised by Professor Ahmed H. Qureshi. I am part of Cognitive Robot Autonomy & Learning (CoRAL) Lab.

Before coming to Purdue, I got my M.S. and B.A. from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, where I worked as a research assistant at the Center for Distributed Robotics advised by Professor Nikolaos Papanikolopoulos.
