I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University, advised by Professor Ahmed H. Qureshi. I am part of the Cognitive Robot Autonomy & Learning (CoRAL) Lab.

Previously, I received my Master in Robotics and Bachelor in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota, where I worked as a research assistant at the Center for Distributed Robotics advised by Professor Nikolaos Papanikolopoulos.
